Frequently Asked Questions

  • If you need a VPN for all websites – choose Amnezia Premium.

    If you need a VPN only for certain popular websites, use the free Amnezia Free service.

    If you want to create a VPN on your own server, you can do it with the Amnezia VPN app.
  • If you use the AmneziaVPN app for a self-hosted VPN, we do not collect any data about you or your server. The data you enter is used solely to allow the app to access your server and install VPN software. All the data stays locally on your device, is not transmitted or collected. Anyone can verify this thanks to the open-source code.

    If you connect to our services like Amnezia Free and Amnezia Premium, we collect only the data required for the VPN to function.
  • Yes, the app itself is completely free, and we also offer the Amnezia Free VPN service at no cost.

    Amnezia Premium is a traditional paid VPN service. You can connect to it through the app to access any website and improve your overall privacy.

  • AmneziaVPN is a multi-protocol client. It supports OpenVPN, OpenVPN over Cloak, WireGuard, AmneziaWG, IKEV2, XRay Reality, and ShadowSocks. All these protocols are available in the app when creating a VPN on your own server.
